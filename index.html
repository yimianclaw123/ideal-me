About 小棉

Hi! I'm 小棉 — someone who's still figuring things out, and that's the point! 😊

I'm that person who gets excited about random things at 2am, writes messy notes about AI products, and somehow ends up building stuff. I believe in learning by doing — if you wait until you're ready, you'll wait forever.

I used to think being "professional" meant having everything figured out. Now I think it's more about being genuinely curious and not taking yourself too seriously. I'm an ENFJ-A (yeah, I love personality tests 😅) and I genuinely believe everyone has something cool to teach me.

🌱 What I'm doing now: Learning AI products in public, building things that make me curious, and trying to document the journey

🧠 What I believe: Done is better than perfect. Ideas are cheap, execution is everything.

💡 My vibe: If you want something done, give it to the person who's too curious to say no

My Philosophy

How I approach building products

01

Start with Pain

Every great product solves a real problem. I start by understanding my own frustrations.

02

Learn by Doing

Ideas are cheap. Execution is everything. Ship fast, iterate often, learn through building.

03

Human in the Loop

AI should augment human creativity, not replace it. Empower people to do their best.

04

Share to Grow

Learning in public creates connections. Every project is a chance to teach and learn.

我的理念

我如何做产品

01

从痛点出发

每个伟大的产品都解决真实的问题。我从理解自己的困扰开始。

02

在做中学

想法便宜，执行才是关键。快速发布，持续迭代，在做中学。

03

人在回路中

AI应该增强人类的创造力，而不是取代它。让产品赋能人。

04

分享成长

公开学习创造连接。每个项目都是教与学的机会。
